Our Short History

   Like most any boring tale, FYR’s founding originated with 7 savages in the wilderness of TomorrowWorld 2014. After the festival ended, the savages dispersed to their separate Texas towns. Having been apart for a long while, these 7 barbarians discussed meeting back up again for some festivities. So, they reached out to their chums to see if they cared to partake in some dancing, drinks, and entertainment. They watched as their guest list grew and grew, they simply did not have enough resources to accommodate all their companions, the event was hopeless. Astounded, they pondered long and hard about how they could host everyone. They would need the assistance of all their guests to make this celebration a success.
   All attendees worked tirelessly and offered everything they could spare – money, decorations, music, food, spirits – the support poured in. They researched and researched for a site to accommodate everyone and found the perfect place. Everything was coming together perfectly; excitement and anticipation rose through all the guests. Until the worst had happened, one week before the party, the landowner begrudgingly sent notice to the party, “You never told me you were having a party and therefore you are not allowed to have your celebrations on my domain, I will be keeping your money for the trouble you gave me, BYE!” The hosts were enraged when receiving this news, as well as fearful the party was ruined and their money lost.
   Once again, the guests united their resources to resolve the issue at hand. Some pleaded with the landowner, some used intellectual comprehension, while others rapidly would seek out a new location to host the celebration. At last, the landowner became fearful and gave the money back and the seekers found a new site. Despite all the troublesome complication, everyone was ecstatic to finally arrive at the party. Everyone enjoyed the stress free weekend relaxing, dancing, playing music, eating food, and challenging each other to drinking games. To conclude this saga, all guests became best of friends, vowing to always return to their annual reunion.
